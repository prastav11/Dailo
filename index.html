About Me

Hello! I’m Prastav Koirala. I'm deeply inspired by minds like Albert Einstein, Isaac Newton, Richard Feynman, and the courageous Stephen Hawking. I love thinking about the universe, time, and the mysteries of life.

One day, a teacher asked me, “Why would you like to be like Kalpana Chawla?” That one question changed my whole perspective. I answered, “So that someday, a teacher like you will ask, why would you like to be like Prastav Koirala?”

Being born as a human gives me the ability to think beyond limits. So why can't we dream big...bigger than this Earth, bigger than the universe, yet smaller than the tiny dot that once exploded to create it?
