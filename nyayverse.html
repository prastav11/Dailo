How to Book a Lawyer (कसरी वकिल बुक गर्ने)

How to Book a Lawyer on Our Website: A Simple Step-by-Step Guide

Book now. Pay only when you consult.

Whether you need legal advice for property, criminal, business, or other matters, booking a lawyer through our website is quick and easy. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Select the Type of Lawyer You Need
    Choose the category of legal help you require, such as Property, Criminal, Business, or Other Legal Matters (e.g., for TikTok content creation or startup advice).
  2. Click "Consult Now"
    Find and click the “Consult Now” button to begin the booking process.
  3. Enter Your Details
    Provide your full name, email address, and contact number in the form.
  4. Choose Your Preferred Lawyer, Date, and Time
    - Select a specific lawyer or choose “No Preference” if you’re open to any available lawyer.
    - Pick a convenient date and time for your consultation.
  5. Write a Specific Message
    Briefly explain your legal needs or questions (e.g., “I need advice on legal issues for my TikTok content” or “I have a startup and need guidance on business laws in Nepal”).
  6. Submit Your Request
    Click the “Send Request” button to submit your booking. This step is completely free!

What Happens Next?

  • Our team will contact you using the phone number you provided.
  • They’ll discuss your needs and confirm the details of your consultation.
  • For general inquiries (like TikTok or startup advice), you can book a free 15-minute consultation with one of our lawyers to get quick legal suggestions tailored to your situation.

Example: Booking a Free 15-Minute Consultation for TikTok or Startup Advice

If you create TikTok content or run a startup in Nepal and need legal guidance, here’s how to book your free consultation:

  • Enter your name, email, and contact number.
  • Select “Other Legal Matter” under Service Needed.
  • Choose “No Preference” for the lawyer.
  • Pick your preferred date and time.
  • Write a message (e.g., “I need legal advice for my TikTok content” or “I want guidance on startup laws in Nepal”).
  • Click “Request” to submit.

Our team will reach out to confirm and set up your free 15-minute consultation with a lawyer who can help with your specific needs.

That’s it! Book your consultation today and get the legal support you need with ease.
